Lynchianism

Escreto por Alessandra Thome Ribeiro Sales. 1 A regular

Beyond being merely a style, Lynchianism carries a poetics of the unexpected: a universe in which the ordinary reveals a fragile surface hiding unsettling and ethereal forces. David Lynch is widely recognized for exploring the instability of reality, blurring the boundaries between dream and reality, identity and duality. His films often employ associative logic, fragmented narratives, and ambiguous symbolism, connecting to Surrealism as well as to explorations of the unconscious.
